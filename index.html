About Weather Forecast Pro

Weather Forecast Pro is a modern, responsive web application that provides accurate weather information for locations worldwide. Our app uses reliable data sources to deliver current conditions and forecasts to help you plan your activities.

Current Weather

Get real-time weather information including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and atmospheric pressure for any city worldwide.

5-Day Forecast

Plan ahead with our detailed 5-day weather forecast that helps you prepare for upcoming weather conditions.

Fully Responsive

Our application works perfectly on all devices - from desktop computers to smartphones and tablets.

Global Coverage

Access weather information for thousands of cities across the globe with our comprehensive database.

How to Use

Step 1

Enter the name of a city in the search box on the Weather page.

Step 2

Click the "Get Weather" button to retrieve current weather data.

Step 3

View the current weather conditions and 5-day forecast for your selected location.
